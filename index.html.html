01 What Data We Collect

The AMTH Bot may collect and temporarily store the following data to perform moderation functions:

  • Discord User IDs and usernames

Data collected is used exclusively to provide moderation functionality. This includes logging moderation actions, enforcing server rules, and maintaining audit trails for server administrators. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.
